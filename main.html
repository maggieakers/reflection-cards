Critique Group Time

Before you click next:

  1. Share your names, pronouns, and programs.
  2. Decide what order you will share in.
  3. Pick one person to share their screen.
  4. Click "pick a card."
  5. Read the card aloud. (Anyone can read aloud.)
  6. Go around the circle, responding aloud to the question on that card.
  7. Repeat the process from Step 4 until 8:30.
